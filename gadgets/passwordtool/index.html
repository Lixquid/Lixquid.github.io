Online Throttled (100 guesses per hour)

Guessing via a web service that successfully throttles guess attempts.

Online Un-Throttled (10 guesses per second)

Guessing via a web service that implements no throttling, or has had its throttling bypassed.

Offline Slow Hash (10,000 guesses per second)

Guessing on an offline database that has its passwords per-user salted, and hashed with a slow hash function with high work factor.

Offline Fast Hash (10,000,000,000 guesses per second)

Guessing on an offline database that has its passwords hashed with a fast hash function with low work factor.

Percentage of Thermodynamic Maximum

Calculates how much energy in the Universe (assuming classical thermodynamics) would be required under completely ideal conditions to guess the password.

Assumes attempting a guess requires the lowest possible amount of work (Boltzman constant multiplied by ambient universe temperature), and that all of the possible mass-energy in the universe is available.

Attempting a guess: 4.4 x 10^-23 Joules

Mass-Energy in Universe: 4 x 10^69 Joules
